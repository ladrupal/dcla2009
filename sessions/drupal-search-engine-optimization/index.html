This session covers Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for Drupal: Which modules you’ll need, how to configure them, and what more you can do to get your site to the top of Google.

Search engine optimization (SEO) is critical consideration that usually comes as an afterthought to most business owners. Get out ahead of the curve by implementing these SEO best practices. You’ve seen our SEO Checklist Module, now learn how to configure your site for maximum performance in Google, Yahoo, and Bing.

This session is designed to be fun and informative. You’ll walk away with a good understanding of all the modules you’ll need to do excellent SEO and you’ll also know exactly how to configure them for best results.

We’ll also have time for Q&A so bring your biggest SEO challenges and let’s solve them together.

Finally, we’ll help you with SEO positioning statements to use when selling site development projects. SEO is a great, ongoing work that most of your clients need.

Pre-requisites: Basic knowledge of installing modules and administrating a Drupal site. No coding necessary.

Session is presented by Ben Finklea, CEO of Drupal SEO company Volacci. Ben is the author of the forthcoming book from Packt publishing called Drupal 6 Search Engine Optimization.

NOTE: This is a sneak preview of the session that I’ll be presenting at Drupalcon in Paris. If you can’t make Drupalcon, get a little taste in LA by attending this session!
