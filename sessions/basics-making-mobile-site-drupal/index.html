51% of the US citizens goes online with his/her mobile device on a weekly basis…

This says enough? Going mobile is gaining importance, and this is where this session will be abou! How to go mobile with your existing Drupal installation, or design your Drupal site specifically for mobile.

The session will guide you through the basics of making a mobile website, the tools, pitfalls, etc …

During the session I will give some practical examples on what can be done with some of the mobile modules in the repositories (maintained by the presenter ;))
- http://drupal.org/project/mobile_tools
- http://drupal.org/project/wurfl
- http://drupal.org/project/siruna

The session will start from a clean Drupal site and end with a mobile… If time, more details can be given on mobile advertising and analytics!!
