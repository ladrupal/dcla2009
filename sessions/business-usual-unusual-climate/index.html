I want to give some tools and encouragement to the small businesses and freelancers out there. I will speak in layman’s terms and hopefully we will have a great time. Also, I throw candy to you in the audience when you reply to my questions.

I will cover topics such as:

  • Project management in a small business or being your own PM as a Sole Proprietor (freelancer)

  • Perseverance in an economic disaster: Keep your chin up, you’ve got a company to run

  • Back to the basics of sales and marketing: Customer referrals and up-selling.

  • Preparing for “the ugly” times: You know they are coming, learn how to prepare.

  • Leadership vs. management: No one wants to work for a stressed out, egotistical, meanie-butt.

  • Incentives: Never underestimate the power of the sticker chart or cookies.
