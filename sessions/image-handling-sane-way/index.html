Image Handling with ImageField and ImageCache – Regain Sanity and Control

Every site needs images, but how do you best achieve this in Drupal? You want to build your Drupal site right and see it grow! Learn how ImageField, ImageCache, CCK and Views2 help your content authors upload and include images easily, while you (the site admin) gain full control over image assets on your site and can reuse and mash up images with Views2.

See also

This session nicely dovetails with Views Demystified, but emphasizes the CCK side of things.

If you don’t know much about CCK, you will also benefit from CCK Demystified.

Modules covered

If you want to follow along, it helps to have these modules installed:
CCK
FileField
ImageField
ImageAPI
ImageCache
Views
Features
Acquia Drupal includes all these modules except for Features.
