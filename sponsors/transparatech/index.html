About

TransparaTech is based in San Diego, CA, and is proud to be sponsoring our northerly sister city of Los Angeles for this summer DrupalCamp. We donated some money, some time, and a video camera to the camp this year so that we can all have a great learning experience among friends. It’s going to be awesome!

We are a limited engagement Drupal Consultancy with a small staff of four, and take on only those projects which we feel are going to be both fun and rewarding to our customers, their clients, and ourselves. Life is too short to do business any other way!

See you around the camp this weekend, and come say hi!
