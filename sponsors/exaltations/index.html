About

Exaltation of Larks specializes in the strategic planning, design, development and maintenance of social networking sites and online communities. Our tools enable people to work and play effectively online through elegant, yet powerful software and services.

For a recent example of our work in virtual worlds, virtual goods and micro-transactions, see our case study, Form Follows Fun: The Making of Digital Dollhouse.

First impressions are important

The first contact anyone has with your business or organization is most likely via your website. We understand that it’s an extension of your business, and in some cases it is your business. We can help you make your website represent how you want your business or organization to be perceived.

How we work

Our success is built on long-term relationships with our clients. We don’t take on new projects unless we feel we share a common vision, and we’ve found that this brings a unique enthusiasm and commitment to what we do. We value direct participation and maintain an intranet where our clients can monitor the development process, post questions and maintain close communications with their website’s developers — integrating customers, clients and partners into the development process.

What's in a name?

The name “exaltation of larks” comes from an old Victorian parlor game that invented many of the collective names we use today, such as a pride of lions and a crash of rhinos. Birds are incredibly social creatures and we chose Exaltation of Larks because of the social nature of the work we do.
