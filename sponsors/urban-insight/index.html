About

Urban Insight is an experienced, creative, and professional technology consulting firm. We plan, build and operate successful websites using web content management systems, including the Drupal framework.

Urban Insight is the technical and editorial team behind the world’s most popular urban planning website, Planetizen, where we use the same technologies that we help our clients implement.

We follow a rigorous, yet flexible software development methodology, and our project management techniques ensure that our clients and our projects are successful.

Services

  • Web Development
  • Drupal Consulting
  • Drupal Training
  • Internet Marketing
  • Technology Infrastructure Consulting

Urban Insight is headquartered in the Metro Arts Building in the walkable Museum Square area of Los Angeles, within easy walking distance of The Grove, Farmer’s Market, and the Los Angeles County Art Museum.
