Renting out the UCI Pub

Note: this is an archived site. Visit us at http://ladrupal.org.
Submitted by Blake Lucchesi on Sat, 2009-07-11 13:36
  • After Hours

Ok so we have been talking about getting the Pub open for all us attendees after the event on Saturday evening. The pub is a great place on campus to unwind, they have some basic pub food and lots of great beers (and a few ciders) to choose from. Follow up here if this is something you all would be interested in seeing this happen. There is a ~$400 fee to reserving it, but if we get enough people to go then its waived so I don't think it'll be an issue.

The pub would easily hold ~150 people and there is lots of outside space right in front of the pub (it's in the middle of the student center).

Let's make this happen!
