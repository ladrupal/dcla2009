Bio

along with chris charlton, and christefano, i am co-manager of the LAdrupal user group and manager of the southern california user group. i helped crystal & chris organize drupalcamp LA 2008 i've been using drupal since mid-2005. i'm a foss freak. i like to expand my drupal network connections on linked in and facebook. twilb

i have organized drupal exhibit booths at open source events:
  • SCaLE 6x
  • Linux World Expo (San Francisco)
  • SCaLE 7x
tech presentations:
  • LA Drupal - various
  • DrupalCamp Los Angeles 2008 - Theming with Zen. FOSS Drupal development in a box.
  • SAND Camp 2009 - FOSS Drupal development in a box.
  • SCaLE 7x - FOSS Drupal development in a box.
i'm co-founder and topmost geek at media done right. i'm a full time dad. i try not to work full time too. i've been in info tech professionally since early 90's. i know lots of tech and wear lots of hats. i like my sailing hat best.
