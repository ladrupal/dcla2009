DrupalCamp LA 2009

Sponsorship

Note: this is an archived site. Visit us at http://ladrupal.org.

The goal of DrupalCampLA is to bring together the fun, smart, and open Drupal minds in the community. People learn, teach, and share. Our style might be similar to a Drupalcon, if not better with each year being free+cheap. Consider this a mini-con. ;-)

Sponsors provide LA Drupal with the support each year needed to put on such an event.

Why sponsor?

If this is your first time sponsoring our annual camp, picture this: You benefit directly from Drupal and its strong community. Help build an even stronger community, resulting in a better Drupal. DrupalCampLA is run completely by volunteers. Last year we spent $4,000 on providing wireless network alone! Venue costs are not small either. We give away prizes each day. We shoot video and offer it free online … well you get the idea. It all costs a lot, and we try to keep attendee costs dramatically low – usually free.

Our sponsors help make it really all happen. Your backing gets us to pay for the necessary costs. You get the idea. :)

If you are a returning sponsor: Thank you!

What do Sponsors get out of it?

Simple. How about this: You support us, we support you. We feel it behooves everyone to know who stepped up to help make this event a reality. We do our best to make sure the community knows you gave back.

Last year we were able to provide sponsor tables, put up their logo where it made sense, and burned-in sponsor names on all videos released from the camp. Each video gets over 100 views a day and we have more recorded content than any other regional Drupal user group around the world!

This year we will have tables for sponsors. We cannot hang anything on the walls, but digital graffiti is encouraged. There are a few more intimate details we’d can share with serious sponsor investments, so please contact us after reviewing our wonderfully priced sponsor packages below.
