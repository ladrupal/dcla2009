Drupalchix conversation - anonymous questions opened up

Note: this is an archived site. Visit us at http://ladrupal.org.
Submitted by rainbreaw on Fri, 2009-08-07 09:50
  • dont send

Drupalists of both genders: the drupalchix community conversation on Sunday at 2pm is going to be a fantastic conversation about ways to encourage and retain more women in the drupal community!

We are also featuring some members of the community on this panel, including Crystal Williams from WorkHabit and Brenda Boggs from Acquia, Nicole Bluto from Intrinsic Web Designs and Rain Breaw from SunRain Productions.

We have just now opened up a method for you to ask questions of the panel without associating your name to the question. The idea behind this is to get people talking honestly without self-censoring for political correctness.

The most effective way to find a solution to the low percentage of women participating is to be honest and open, so that’s what we intend to do!

Please submit anonymous questions to make this a better conversation! Questions can be read and submitted here: Questions for the Drupalchix Panel

Submit your question here: www.drupalcampla.com/node/add/drupalchixpanel

Read more about the Drupalchix Conversation and it’s participants here:
http://2009.drupalcampla.com/sessions/women-drupal-please-stand-panel-featuring-exceptional-women-drupal-discussing-how-encourage

See you in the community room on Sunday!
